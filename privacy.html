Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

VocaiWords ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Apple ID Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID and optionally your email address (if you choose to share it).
  • User Profile: Language preferences, learning level, interests, and learning goals you provide.
  • Learning Data: Words you save, learning progress, quiz results, and statistics.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App features you use, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, and app version.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the VocaiWords service
  • Personalize your learning experience with AI-generated content
  • Sync your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Process subscription purchases and manage your account
  • Send important service updates and support communications
  • Analyze app usage to improve features and performance

Data Storage and Security

  • iCloud Storage: Your learning data is stored securely in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework.
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  • Access Control: Only you can access your data through your Apple ID authentication.

Third-Party Services

We Use:

  • Apple Sign In: For secure authentication
  • Apple CloudKit: For data synchronization across your devices
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management
  • OpenAI API: For AI-powered content generation (definitions, examples, stories)

We Do NOT:

  • Share your personal information with advertisers
  • Use tracking cookies or analytics that identify you personally
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Display advertisements in the app

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export your learning data
  • Opt-Out: Disable iCloud sync in app settings

Children's Privacy

VocaiWords is suitable for users aged 4 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Users

Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, which follows Apple's data residency policies based on your Apple ID country/region settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: privacy@vocaiwords.com
