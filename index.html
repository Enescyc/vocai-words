Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VocaiWords

How do I upgrade my subscription?
Open the app, navigate to Profile ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Choose your plan (Learner or Master). You can upgrade anytime, and changes take effect immediately.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Sign in with the same Apple ID, then go to Profile ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Restore Purchases. Your subscription will sync automatically via iCloud.
How does AI word enrichment work?
Our advanced AI analyzes each word and generates contextual definitions, real-world example sentences, pronunciation guides, related vocabulary, and difficulty assessments tailored to your learning level.
Can I sync my progress across devices?
Yes! Sign in with Apple and your words, progress, and subscription automatically sync across all your iOS and macOS devices via iCloud.
How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS: Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí VocaBuddy ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What languages are supported?
VocaBuddy supports 17+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Turkish, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Russian, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, and Polish.
How does spaced repetition work?
The app tracks your learning progress and schedules review sessions at optimal intervals to move words from short-term to long-term memory, ensuring lasting retention.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! Your saved words and progress are available offline. AI features (enrichment, story generation, recommendations) require an internet connection.
What's the difference between Learner and Master plans?
Learner gives you 50 AI enrichments, 10 stories, and 20 recommendations per month. Master provides unlimited access to all features, plus priority support and early access to new features.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely! We use Sign in with Apple for enhanced privacy, all data is encrypted, synced via iCloud, and we never share your information with third parties. No ads, no tracking.
